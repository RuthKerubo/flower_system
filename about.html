Lily

Lily is a feminine given name directly derived from lily, the ower. In the United States, “Lily” became one of the top-100 names for newborn girls in 2002 and reached a rank of 18 by 2009. hibiscus

White Rose

White rose symbolic history: White roses symbolically go way back in time. The rose is about 35 million years old. The ancient Romans and Greeks mentioned white roses in their literature. Roses are associated with “paradise” according to the Korans and also in the middle ages roses were used in love and poetry.

Dahlia

2019 is the Year of the Dahlia 2019 is the Year of the Dahlia. These flamboyant, colorful plants yield gorgeous blooms from midsummer through autumn, when many plants are best their best. In colder zones, you’ll need to dig up and store the tubers before the first hard frost if you wish to grow them as perennials.
